    Worst LCP pages
    Largest Contentful Paint (s)
    Pages where the biggest visible element takes the longest to paint. Above 2.5s Google calls it slow and ranking starts taking a hit. Compress images, defer offscreen JS, lean on a CDN.

    Cumulative Layout Shift
    Pages where elements jump around the most while loading. Users tap the wrong thing; Google penalizes anything above 0.1. Usually fixed by reserving space for images and ads with explicit width/height.

    Share of business-hour calls your team picked up (Mon-Fri 8am–6pm PT, excluding US holidays). Below 90% means routing or staffing needs attention — every missed call is a paid lead that may not call back. After-hours calls are tracked separately and don't drag this number.
